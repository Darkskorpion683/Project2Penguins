Classification of pengiuns from the Palmer Archipelago

After scoping throught several datasets I came across this one which has to do with penguin from a stretch of islands near antartica.The goal in this project is to predict if a penguin belongs to a certain island.Hopefully can can correctly predict

The dataset was foundd on kaggle:https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/parulpandey/palmer-archipelago-antarctica-penguin-data

The columns are:

  • Species
  • island
  • culmen length
  • culmen depth
  • flipper length
  • body mass

The culmen is "the upper ridge of a bird's beak" (definition from Oxford Languages).
